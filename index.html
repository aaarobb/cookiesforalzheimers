Our Mission

1

Community Engagement

Building and engaging with a compassionate community dedicated to supporting Alzheimer's research.

2

Research Funding

Contributing to Alzheimer's research initiatives that bring us closer to effective treatments. Primarily donates to regional research centers.

3

Awareness

Educating the public about Alzheimer's disease, its molecular origins, its impact, and a general understanding of the field. We aim to breakdown technical papers in simpler language.

4

Compassion

Creating moments of connection for those living with Alzheimer's through the simple, meaningful act of sharing homemade cookies.
